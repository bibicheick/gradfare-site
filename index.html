Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't

One platform replaces your spreadsheets, email chains, and paper tickets.

⏱️
Live Countdown Timer
A beautiful countdown on both admin dashboard and student portal builds excitement. Days, hours, minutes, seconds until the big moment. Try it live ↓
📊
Smart Data Import
Upload from Excel, CSV, Google Sheets, or paste directly. GradFare auto-detects 40+ column name variations — however your registrar formats the data, it just works.
🎫
Ticket Allowance System
Set a default per event, override per student. Each graduate manages their own guest list through a personal portal — no staff bottleneck.
📱
Student Self-Service Portal
Every student gets a unique link — no app, no account. They add guests, get instant QR tickets, and manage invitations anytime from any device.
📷
QR Camera Check-in
Staff point their phone camera at guest QR codes. Instant confirmation with guest name, student, and event. Multiple staff scan simultaneously.
🪑
Seating Arrangements
Create venue sections with capacity limits. Assign students with real-time fill tracking. No more over-booked sections or confused guests.
📋
Ceremony Program Builder
Build your full agenda with speakers, music, awards, and timing. Reorder with one click. Total duration calculated automatically.
🖨️
Print-Ready PDF Tickets
Beautiful A6 tickets with QR codes, guest names, and seat assignments. Print one ticket or batch-print thousands in a single click.
🎨
Custom School Branding
4 built-in themes plus custom school colors. Add your logo, name, and banner image. Students see YOUR brand, not a generic tool.
